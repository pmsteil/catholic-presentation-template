    Catholic Social Teaching

    Seven Principles

    1. Life & Dignity

    Human life is sacred and dignity is the foundation of moral vision

    2. Family & Community

    People have a right and duty to participate in society

    3. Rights & Responsibilities

    Every person has fundamental rights that must be protected

    4. Option for the Poor

    The moral test of society is how we treat the most vulnerable

    5. Dignity of Work

    Work is more than a way to make a living; it's a form of participation in creation

    6. Solidarity

    We are one human family regardless of national, racial, ethnic, economic, and ideological differences

    7. Care for Creation

    We show our respect for the Creator by our stewardship of creation
